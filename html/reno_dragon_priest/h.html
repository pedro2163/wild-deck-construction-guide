Reno Dragon Priest

Index

You can make this deck without Cleric of Scales but it is a really important card. Maybe you should consider the non-dragon version if you miss it.


Once you have all the core cards you need to make some choices:

Choose one

  • Forbidden Words
  • Shadow Word: Pain
  • Holy Smite

Choose one

  • Dragonfire Potion
  • Soul Mirror

Choose maximum one

  • Big'ol Whelp
  • Loatheb
  • Skeletal Dragon

Choose the rest

  • Novice Engineer
  • Dirty Rat
  • Twilight Whelp
  • Potion of Madness
  • Holy Smite
  • Seance
  • Breath of the Infinite
  • Mass Dispell
  • Emperor Thaurissan

Cards to consider

  • Greater Healing Potion
  • Holy Nova
  • Acidic Swamp Ooze
  • Renew

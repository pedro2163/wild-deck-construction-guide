    Cards to consider in the Quest package

    This package is the less good. It can be hard to win games sometimes with this one. You have two choices: 1, go with N'zoth the Corruptor and full of big deathrattle minions or 2, try to finish quickly the quest, Amara Warden of Hope will be a second Reno Jackson and you will try to win with the normal combo. So, just type deathrattle in the Hearthstone search bar and add anything you want. Just be sure to have enough deathrattle minions to fill your quest!

      Other cards

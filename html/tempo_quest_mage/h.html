Wild Construction Deck Guide

Tempo Quest Mage

Index

I would recommend to avoid building this deck if you don't have Licensed Adventurer but you can always replace it with Shimmering Tempest or Tome of Intellect.

In the Budget version you can replace 1x Shooting Star with any card in this list.

In the Budget version you can have any one of the two giants!

Once you have all the core cards you need to make some choices:

Choose two

  • 2x Starscryer
  • 1x Arcane Intellect
  • Stargazer Luna
  • Chenvaala

Other cards to consider

  • Sky Gen'ral Kragg
  • 1x Shooting Star
  • 1x Flame Ward
  • 1x Banana Buffon
  • Archmage Vargoth

Flexible cards

In the final version you can play with three giants if you want. It is better vs Aggro decks but can be difficult vs Combo/Control decks.
